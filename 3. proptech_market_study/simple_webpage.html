Project Description: PropTech Market Study Dashboard

This dashboard was designed to provide a comprehensive analysis of the PropTech (Property Technology) market, with a focus on investment opportunities in Smart City Concepts (SCC) for venture capital clients. The app is built using Dash and offers interactive data visualizations and insights to assist in making informed investment decisions.

This app is designed to empower decision-makers in the VC space with interactive tools, ensuring they have the data and insights needed to make strategic investment decisions in the rapidly evolving PropTech sector.

Tools: Python, Plotly, Dash, Render
